## 👁️ About HaloRift

**HaloRift** is an open-source cybersecurity research and offensive security organization. We focus on uncovering vulnerabilities, understanding attacker mindsets, and building educational tools that help security professionals, sysadmins, and students defend against modern threat vectors.

Our core philosophy is simple: You cannot defend against what you do not understand. We build the tools that simulate real-world attacks to fortify real-world defenses.

---

## 🎯 Featured Research & Tools

### 🎣 [The Social Engineering Toolkit](https://github.com/HaloRift/The-Social-Engineering-Toolkit)
A comprehensive collection of social engineering frameworks designed for educational and ethical awareness training. 
- **Purpose:** Helps organizations simulate phishing attacks, harvest awareness metrics, and train employees against manipulation tactics.
- **Capabilities:** Credential harvesting simulations, deceptive routing, and OSINT vector mapping.
- **Impact:** Highly cloned and utilized by the community to understand the mechanics of social engineering.

> ⚠️ **Strictly Ethical Use:** *All tools published by HaloRift are strictly for authorized, educational, and defensive purposes. The authors are not responsible for any misuse or illegal activities.*
